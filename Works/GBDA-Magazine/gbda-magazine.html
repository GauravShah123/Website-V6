Introduction

Introducing "Designed Worlds," my very own magazine born out of my Intro to Design course (GBDA 101). Before this class, I thought design was all about pretty visuals and vague messages. But this course transformed my perspective. In this magazine, I share my top five takeaways, revealing that design goes beyond logos and posters. Everything around us has purposeful design, shaped by cultural contexts and individual experiences. I also explore the power of feedback, the importance of user experience, and the liberating art of breaking design rules. I hope you enjoy reading it just as much as I did creating it.
